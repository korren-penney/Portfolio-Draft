Practice in Action

How my teaching framework is applied through real learning experiences.

Applying My Framework

This section demonstrates how my teaching framework is put into practice. Each artifact reflects a specific aspect of my approach, showing how my decisions around design, learner support, and technology create applied, meaningful learning experiences.

Approach to Learning
Learning Through Reflection

Pecha Kucha Presentation

I designed this assignment to support concise, reflective engagement with research. It required synthesizing ideas and connecting them to lived experience.

This reflects my belief that learning is strengthened through reflection, interpretation, and active engagement.

Design & Technology in Practice
Hands-On Digital Learning

Beyond the Browser Podcast

I designed this project to move beyond traditional assignments by engaging learners in a creative, applied format that builds both critical thinking and communication skills.

This reflects my belief that technology should support meaningful engagement and position learners as active creators.

Learner Support
Structured Guidance

Post-Secondary Success Workshop

This workshop was designed to support learners navigating transition by providing clear structure, expectations, and practical strategies.

This reflects my belief that supportive, well-structured environments are essential for learner confidence and success.

Accessibility & Inclusion
Inclusive Practice

Designing for Access and Confidence

I design learning experiences that reduce barriers and provide multiple ways for learners to engage with content.

This reflects my belief that inclusion is created through intentional design, not assumed.

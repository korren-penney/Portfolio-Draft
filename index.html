Building My Practice as an Adult Educator

A reflective journey through the Master of Adult Education (MAdEd) program at Saint Francis Xavier University (StFX).

Welcome

This portfolio presents my growth as an adult educator through reflection, evidence, and critical analysis. It is organized using a house-building metaphor to illustrate the ongoing development of my practice.


Each section represents a critical phase of constructing a house, from the underlying philosophical foundation to the final structural framework and the evidence of the work within.

My Focus

My portfolio represents my development as an adult educator who supports adult learners in navigating transitions by making technology education accessible, practical, and meaningful, with a focus on applied, career-oriented learning that leads to personal and professional growth.
